Coding and Game idea
Puzzle platformer
2023

Gameplay

The player progresses through a series of puzzles where they need to interact with past versions of themselves from previous runs and use them to solve the puzzles.

Reflection

This was a relatively small project. A lot of my time was spent creating the movement system, as I wanted to try my hand at developing my own physics with collisions and related mechanics. Due to time constraints, I opted to use a mix of Unity's collision system and only coded the forces myself.

I believe the game has a lot of potential with a few tweaks to improve the game feel and by adding more diverse levels. The current set of levels primarily tests the platforming abilities of the player, and even that in only a limited way.
